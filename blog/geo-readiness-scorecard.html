GEO Readiness Scorecard: Are AI Engines Citing Your Brand?

By Jeff Harmon | Founder, Harmon Content

Published: April 5, 2026

Take this 10-question scorecard to find out if your content is visible to ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude — or if your brand is invisible to 37% of searchers who use AI tools.

For each question, give yourself the points that apply. Total your score and check the results below.

The Scorecard (100 Points Total)

1. Schema Markup (15 points)

  • Your site has no schema markup: 0 pts
  • You have basic schema (just Organization or Article): 5 pts
  • You use FAQ, HowTo, or Product schema in addition to Article: 10 pts
  • You have FAQ + Article + Organization + HowTo + BreadcrumbList schemas: 15 pts

2. Answer-First Content (15 points)

  • Your blog posts start with long introductions: 0 pts
  • You sometimes answer the core question in the first paragraph: 5 pts
  • Your homepage and key service pages lead with direct answers: 10 pts
  • Most of your content uses answer-first structure consistently: 15 pts

3. Fact Density (15 points)

  • Your content uses lots of marketing language ("innovative," "cutting-edge," "game-changing"): 0 pts
  • You occasionally include statistics: 5 pts
  • Most articles include 3+ sources with hyperlinks: 10 pts
  • Your content is data-dense: specific numbers, named sources, expert quotes, case studies: 15 pts

4. Third-Party Mentions (15 points)

  • No one on the internet mentions your brand outside your own website: 0 pts
  • You have a few social media mentions: 5 pts
  • You have guest posts, forum answers, or Quora answers: 10 pts
  • You're regularly mentioned on Reddit, industry blogs, newsletters, and guest publications: 15 pts

5. Topic Authority (10 points)

  • Your blog covers random, unrelated topics: 0 pts
  • You have a few related articles: 3 pts
  • You have a content cluster (4+ interconnected posts on one topic): 6 pts
  • You have multiple topic clusters with clear interlinking: 10 pts

6. Content Freshness (5 points)

  • Your most recent blog post is older than 12 months: 0 pts
  • Your last post was 6-12 months ago: 2 pts
  • You publish monthly and update old posts quarterly: 3 pts
  • You publish 2+ times/month with "Last Updated" dates on all content: 5 pts

7. AI Engine Optimization (10 points)

  • You only optimize for traditional SEO: 0 pts
  • You've heard of GEO but haven't implemented it: 3 pts
  • You've started optimizing some content for AI engines: 7 pts
  • You actively optimize for both SEO and GEO across all content: 10 pts

8. Brand Entity Profile (5 points)

  • No About page, no founder bio, no consistent brand info across platforms: 0 pts
  • You have an About page but it's thin: 2 pts
  • You have an About page, founder bio, and consistent brand mentions: 3 pts
  • You have entity optimization (About page, bios, directory consistency, Google Business Profile): 5 pts

9. AI Citation Testing (5 points)

  • You've never tested if AI engines cite your brand: 0 pts
  • You've tried once out of curiosity: 2 pts
  • You check monthly and track results: 5 pts

10. Repurposed Content (5 points)

  • You only publish on your own website: 0 pts
  • You cross-post to 1 other platform (e.g., Medium): 2 pts
  • You repurpose blog content into LinkedIn posts, X threads, and emails: 5 pts

How to Calculate Your Score

Add up your points across all 10 questions. Then check the results below.

Results: What Your Score Means

0–30 Points: Critical — You're Invisible to AI

Your brand is currently invisible to AI engines. Every competitor who optimizes for GEO will outrank you in AI-generated answers. You're missing out on 37% of searchers who use AI as their first search tool — and that number is growing fast.

Priority fixes: Add schema markup, rewrite content with answer-first structure, start posting on Reddit and Quora, build a topic cluster.

31–50 Points: High Risk — AI Engines Barely See You

Priority fixes: Increase fact density (add data, stats, citations), boost third-party mentions (guest posts, forums), test AI citation weekly to track progress.

51–70 Points: Moderate — You're Building Authority

You're on the right track. AI engines recognize you for some queries, but you're not yet a dominant voice. With focused optimization, you can reach the 80+ point level within 60-90 days.
